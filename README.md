## üöÄ How to Use This Guide

### For Interview Preparation

1. **Assess Your Level**
   - Identify your target role and experience level
   - Start with your corresponding section
   - Review fundamentals even if you're experienced

2. **Study Strategically**
   - Read each question and answer thoroughly
   - Understand the diagrams and workflows
   - Note the key takeaways and frameworks
   - Practice explaining concepts in your own words

3. **Practice Delivery**
   - Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
   - Practice articulating answers out loud
   - Prepare follow-up question responses
   - Time yourself to ensure concise answers

4. **Focus on Understanding**
   - Don't memorize - understand the concepts
   - Be able to explain "why" not just "what"
   - Connect concepts to real-world scenarios
   - Prepare to discuss trade-offs and alternatives

### For Skill Development

- Use as a reference guide for day-to-day work
- Share knowledge with your team
- Build projects based on the architectures
- Stay updated with the latest developments
